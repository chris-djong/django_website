Information
Stock
The Stock you bought. In case it is not listed just create a new one.
Amount
How many stocks you bought/sold.
Label
This label is used to sort the stocks in your Overview and manage your diversification. In order to add mutiple labels to a stock use the a "/". Example: Cybersecurity/Data Mining/Biomedical
Date
The date you performed the transaction. This date is used to retrieve the price.
Price
Not required. The price per stock that you payed. In case you add your own price, the program wil automatically overwrite the value obtained using Date.
Fees
The amount of fees you had to pay on top of the given price. You can enter both a constant and a linear term dependant on the price. Note that the sell fees will be overwritten once you enter the sell transaction.
Lower/Upper Alert
Obtain an automatic alert email whenever the price is lower/higher as a certain value. Hotmail.com emails will block our email by their privacy settings however.
