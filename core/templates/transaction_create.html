Stock:The Stock you bought. In case it is not listed just create a new one.
Amount:How many stocks you bought. In case you sold stock simply use a negative value.
Label:This label is used to sort the stocks in your Overview and manage your diversification. In order to add mutiple labels to a stock use the a "/". Example: Cybersecurity/Data Mining/Biomedical
Date:The date of the transaction. This date is used to retrieve the price.
Price:Not required. The price per stock that you payed. In case you add your own price, the program wil automatically overwrite the value obtained using Date.
Fees: The amount of fees you had to pay on top of the price. You can specify both a constant term and a linear term dependant on the price you paid for the stocks. Note that in case you create a sell transaction (negative amount) than the Fees are the sell fees and you are not required to enter the expected sell fees.
